Billing FAQs

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

Ans: We accept all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and other

payment methods depending on your region.

Q: Will I receive an invoice for my purchase?

Ans: Yes, an invoice is automatically sent to your email after completing the

transaction.

Q: How can I update my billing information?

Ans: You can update your billing information by logging into your account,

navigating to the “Billing” section, and making the necessary changes.

Q: Can I change my billing cycle?

Ans: Yes, you can switch between monthly and annual billing cycles in your

account settings. Changes will take effect on the next billing date.

Q: Do you offer refunds for billing errors?

Ans: Yes, if you were incorrectly charged, please contact customer support

within [X days] for a refund or adjustment.

Q: Why was my payment declined?

Ans: Payments may be declined due to insufficient funds, expired cards, or

incorrect billing details. Please check with your bank or update your information.

Q: Do you charge any additional fees or taxes?

Ans: Taxes or fees may apply depending on your region and local regulations.

These will be displayed at checkout.
