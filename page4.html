Shipping/return FAQs

Q: What are the available shipping options?

Ans: We offer standard, expedited, and next-day shipping. You can select your

preferred option during checkout.

Q: How long does standard shipping take?

Ans: Standard shipping typically takes 3–7 business days, depending on

your location.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?

Ans: Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping times

and rates vary depending on the destination.

Q: What is your return policy?

Ans: We accept returns within 30 days of purchase, provided the item is in

its original condition and packaging.

Q: Do you offer free shipping?

Ans: Yes, if you were incorrectly charged, please contact customer support

within [X days] for a refund or adjustment.

Q: What if my package arrives damaged?

Ans: If your package arrives damaged, please contact us immediately with

photos of the damage, and we’ll assist you with a replacement or refund.

Q: How long does it take to process a return?

Ans: Once we receive your returned item, it typically takes 5–10 business

days to process the return and issue a refund to your original payment method.

Q: Who pays for return shipping?

Ans: In most cases, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs.

However, if the return is due to our error (e.g., wrong item sent), we will cover the shipping costs.
